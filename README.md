Chocolate Sales Dataset (CSV)
This dataset contains detailed transactional records of global chocolate product sales from July 2022 to December 2025. It is designed for sales performance analysis, trend forecasting, and individual/regional contribution evaluation in Power BI or other analytics tools.
Key Columns and Descriptions:

Date – Transaction date (format: YYYY-MM-DD)
Product – Full product name (e.g., "50% Dark Bites", "Smooth Silky Caramel", "White Chocolate Eclairs")
Amount – Sales amount in USD for the transaction ($)
Boxes Shipped – Quantity of boxes sold in the transaction
Country – Customer/sales country (e.g., Australia, UK, India, USA)
Sales Person – Name of the responsible sales representative (e.g., Mallorie Waber, Dennison Cross, Andria Kimpto)

Key Metrics Derived from This Data (in Power BI):

Total Sales: ~$19.79M across all periods
Strong YoY Growth: +54.3%
Top Performing Country: Australia
Top Performing Sales Person: Mallorie Waber
Best-Selling Product Category: Premium dark chocolate variants (especially "50% Dark Bites")

Usage Recommendations:
Ideal for building interactive dashboards focused on sales trends, product performance, regional comparison, and sales team evaluation. Supports time intelligence, decomposition analysis, and top-N rankings.
